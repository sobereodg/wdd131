Gaming Hub - Site Plan

Site Name

Gaming Hub: CODM Zone – This name reflects a focused community and resource center for Call of Duty Mobile players. It's designed to be a one-stop hub for everything related to CODM, including guides, updates, and multiplayer strategies.

Optional domain availability: codmhub.com

Site Purpose

The site provides a central platform for Call of Duty Mobile gamers to stay up to date with game news, find tutorials, review gear recommendations, connect with other players, and join online events or tournaments. It also offers tips for beginners and strategy guides for advanced players.

Scenarios

  • What are the best loadouts for ranked matches in COD Mobile?
  • Where can I find upcoming tournament schedules and how do I register?

Color Schema

  • #00c3ff – Used for headings, links, and highlights (represents tech and energy).
  • #ffb400 – Used for accents, buttons, and calls to action (draws attention and adds contrast).
  • #1f1f1f – Background color to create a gaming-themed, immersive dark mode look.

Typography

  • Orbitron – Used for main headings to give a futuristic, high-tech vibe.
  • Roboto – Used for body text for clean, readable content.
